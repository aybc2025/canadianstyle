5.01 Basic Rules

Fundamental principles for expressing numbers in Canadian government writing

General Principle

Write out numbers from one to ten; use numerals for 11 and above. However, many exceptions apply depending on context.

Basic Number Writing Rules

  • One to ten: Write in words (one, two, three, etc.)
  • 11 and above: Use numerals (11, 12, 25, 100)
  • Round numbers: Large round numbers can be written in words (one million, two billion)
  • Beginning of sentence: Always write out numbers at the start of sentences
