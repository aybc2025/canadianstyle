15.02 Canadian Places

Canadian place names follow specific conventions that reflect our bilingual heritage and diverse geography.

Official Canadian Place Names

  • Cities and towns: Use official municipal names
  • Provinces/territories: Use full names in formal writing
  • Geographic features: Follow Gazetteer of Canada
  • Consistency: Maintain same form throughout document

Regional Designations

The Maritimes

New Brunswick, Nova Scotia, and Prince Edward Island

Atlantic Canada

Includes the Maritimes plus Newfoundland and Labrador

The Prairies

Manitoba, Saskatchewan, and Alberta

Central Canada

Ontario and Quebec (sometimes called "Eastern Canada")

The North

Yukon, Northwest Territories, and Nunavut
